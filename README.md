# Oil-Painting-of-Flowers---Reflection-Project
Reflection on Floral Oil Painting" is a collaborative project by Yang Longhai杨龙海, Li Yixuan李宜轩 and Liu Yuanyi刘媛依. Using oil painting as a medium, the project aims to capture the inner spirit of flowers rather than the precision of their physical forms. The brushwork does not pursue meticulous detail; instead, it embraces abstraction—color blocks bleed into one another, and contours evolve organically in a state of flux, approaching the ineffable aura of blossoms that eludes precise depiction.
 
The work documents the ephemeral traces left by different flowers on the canvas: from the fiery bloom of peonies to the understated elegance of daisies. Each stroke becomes a meditation on transience. Here, flowers cease to be mere still objects for observation; they transform into a fissure leading to deeper contemplation—revealing how beauty arrives, and how it is perpetually in a state of farewel
 
This creation invites viewers to look beyond the surface of color, to discern the unresolved inquiries about life, impermanence, and the enduring dialogue between humanity and nature. For those who cherish oil painting and the natural world, this may just be a place worthy of lingering in for a long time.
