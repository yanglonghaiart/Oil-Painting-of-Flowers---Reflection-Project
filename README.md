# Oil-Painting-of-Flowers---Reflection-Project
The "Oil - Painting of Flowers --- Reflection Project" is jointly promoted by Yang Longhai杨龙海  , Li Yixuan李宜轩, Liu Yuanyi刘媛依 and others. The project focuses on using the form of oil painting to show the beauty and connotation of flowers. Through delicate brushstrokes and rich colors, it presents the postures of different flowers and guides viewers to reflect on the relationship between nature and humanity. Each work is a profound reflection on life, beauty and transience. From delicate roses to elegant daisies, one can not only see the external beauty of the flowers, but also understand their philosophical meaning through the paintings. It is the interwoven result of art and thought, bringing a unique experience to those who love oil painting and nature.
